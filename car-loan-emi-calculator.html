FAQs about Car Loan EMI

What is EMI?
EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It's the monthly payment combining interest and principal.
How is EMI calculated?
EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1], where P is loan amount, R is monthly interest rate, and N is total payments.
Does EMI change over time?
For fixed-rate loans, EMI remains constant. Only the split between interest and principal changes.
