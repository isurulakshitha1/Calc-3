FAQs – Used Car Loans

Are used car loan interest rates higher?
Yes, they are typically 1–2% higher due to increased lender risk on pre-owned vehicles.
What down payment is recommended?
We suggest at least 20% of the vehicle price to reduce EMI and interest costs.
Can I prepay a used car loan?
Most lenders allow early repayment with minimal or no prepayment penalties.
